Key Findings

Insights derived from exploratory data analysis and model interpretation

🦠

COVID-19 Shock

April–May 2020 lockdown caused unemployment to spike from 8.3% to 23.5% — a 183% surge in just 60 days.

📍

Regional Disparity

States like Tripura and Haryana show 3× higher unemployment than states like Gujarat and Odisha — rural vs urban divide.

🗓️

Seasonal Cycles

April–June historically sees higher unemployment due to academic transitions and reduced agricultural activity.

📊

Labour Participation Key

Labour participation rate is the strongest predictor of unemployment — drops in LPR signal economic withdrawal.

📈

Slow Recovery

Post-COVID recovery stabilized at 11.8% — still 42% above the pre-COVID baseline, indicating structural damage.

🌾

Policy Recommendation

Target skill-development in high-unemployment states; strengthen MGNREGA buffers for seasonal employment gaps.
